Give It a Try

The studio also offers introductory courses which are designed to give new students an opportunity to try a musical instrument so that they (and their parents) can determine if formal lessons are something they would like to pursue. Everything needed is supplied so there is no need to procure music or an instrument. Courses usually consist of 12 half hour lessons and are limited to 4–6 students. Give It a Try courses have been offered regularly to students at Grace Christian School in Telford, PA. Homeschooled students can access the courses at GCS. If you are interested in having your group “Give It a Try”, contact Mr. Daam to discuss the possibilities.
