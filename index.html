Welcome!

Coda Music Studio is a single instructor, private residence–based music studio located in Telford, Pennsylvania. The Studio offers private and group lessons for piano, drums and other instruments as well as for voice, music theory and other music related skills. Please refer to the lessons page for more information on the types of lessons offered.

What Does Coda Mean?

co·da \kō'də n. [Italian, from Latin cauda, tail.] Music. A passage formally ending a composition or section.



Mission Statement

To foster the appreciation, understanding and enjoyment of many types of music through the development of each student’s potential in musicianship, self-confidence and performance.
